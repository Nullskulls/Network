Why networking?

A hacker's best tool is how they network. A good hacker networks with everyone and everything. One could say that it's a hacker's best friend. Networking can be many things.. Communicating with people or just literally connecting to stuff. Why don't you push the limits of what defines a network..

What is Hack Club?
Hack Club is the world's biggest community of teenage makers! We're a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, supported by folks like GitHub and donors who believe in young builders. We're fully transparent and here to help you ship.
Who can join?
Anyone 13 to 18 (inclusive)! If that's you, you're in. YSWS is part of Hack Club and runs on the same spirit: build cool stuff, connect with others, get supported.
Wait, grants? You give money to build stuff?
Yes! Ship a networking related app and you can get a grant to help you build and connect. We want to help you make things that bring people together.
How do I get started?
Share what you're making and apply for a grant. It's that simple! Submit your project and share with the community what you built!
